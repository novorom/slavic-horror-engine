YouTube Community Tab Posts Guide
===================================

Community Tab Post Types:

1. Video Updates (Post new video releases)
   - Text: "🌙 Nuevo terror eslavo disponible: [video title]"
   - Add video thumbnail
   - Link to new video
   - Hashtags: #TerrorEslavo #CuentosDeTerror
   - Timing: Immediately after video upload

2. Polls (Engagement)
   - Question: "¿Cuál es tu monstruo eslavo favorito?"
   - Options: Baba Yaga, Leshy, Rusalka, Viy
   - Duration: 7 days
   - Image: Collage of monsters

3. Images (Behind the scenes/Art)
   - Share channel assets
   - Monster artwork
   - Thumbnail creation process
   - Caption: "El arte detrás del terror..."

4. Questions (Community engagement)
   - "¿Qué leyenda eslava te gustaría ver?"
   - "¿Cuál historia te dio más miedo?"
   - "¿Conoces otros monstruos eslavos?"
   - Encourage comments and discussion

5. Updates (Channel milestones)
   - "¡Gracias por [X] suscriptores!"
   - "Nuevo playlist disponible: [name]"
   - "Mejoras en el canal..."

Post Schedule:
- Daily: New video announcement
- Weekly: Poll or question
- Monthly: Milestone updates
- Special: Holiday themed posts

Engagement Tips:
- Post consistently (daily/weekly)
- Use emojis for visual appeal
- Include relevant hashtags
- Respond to comments
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Celebrate milestones with community
