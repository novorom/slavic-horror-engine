YouTube Cards Template Guide
==============================

Card Timing (max 5 cards per video):
- Card 1: 0:05 - Early engagement
- Card 2: 0:15 - Mid-video engagement  
- Card 3: 0:25 - Related content
- Card 4: 0:35 - Playlist promotion
- Card 5: 0:40 - Final call-to-action

Card Types:

1. Video Link Cards
   - Link to related horror stories
   - Text: "Historia similar: [monster name]"
   - Teaser text: "¿Qué te espera en el bosque?"
   - Custom timing: When relevant monster mentioned

2. Playlist Cards
   - Link to "Cuentos de Terror Eslavo" playlist
   - Text: "Más historias de [monster type]"
   - Teaser: "Colección completa de terror"
   - Timing: After main story revealed

3. Channel Cards
   - Link to channel main page
   - Text: "Suscríbete para más terror"
   - Teaser: "Nuevo video cada día"
   - Timing: End of video

4. Poll Cards (for engagement)
   - Question: "¿Cuál es tu monstruo eslavo favorito?"
   - Options: Baba Yaga, Leshy, Rusalka, Viy
   - Timing: Mid-video when monster introduced

Card Text Templates:
- "Mira también: [related video title]"
- "Colección completa: [playlist name]"
- "¿Te gustaría más historias de [monster]?"
- "El terror continúa en [video title]"
- "Suscríbete para no perderte nada"

Card Best Practices:
- Keep text short and engaging
- Use custom thumbnails for cards
- Don't overwhelm viewers with too many cards
- Time cards to match video content
- Test on mobile devices
- Use consistent branding

Card Teaser Text Examples:
- "El secreto del bosque..."
- "Lo que nadie te contó..."
- "¿Te atreves a ver esto?"
- "La verdad sobre [monster]..."
- "Algo acecha en las sombras..."

How to Add Cards:
1. YouTube Studio → Content
2. Select video → Edit
3. Cards tab
4. Add card at desired timestamp
5. Choose card type and content
6. Customize text and timing
7. Save changes

Automated Cards:
- Can set default card templates
- Apply to similar videos
- Customize per video for relevance
