YouTube Comment Moderation Guide
================================

Comment Moderation Settings:

1. Automatic Filters (YouTube Studio → Settings → Community)
   - Block potentially inappropriate comments: ON
   - Hold potentially inappropriate comments for review: ON
   - Block spam: ON
   - Hide comments from users with hidden history: ON

2. Blocked Words (Spanish & English)
   Spam:
   - "subscribe to my channel"
   - "check out my video"
   - "free subscribers"
   - "get more views"
   - "follow for follow"
   - "sígueme y te sigo"
   - "suscríbete a mi canal"
   
   Inappropriate:
   - Profanity and offensive language
   - Hate speech terms
   - Personal attacks
   - Harassment

3. Moderation Levels
   - Strict: For new channel (protect brand)
   - Medium: After 1000 subscribers (balance)
   - Light: After 10000 subscribers (community trust)

Comment Approval Workflow:
1. Review held comments daily
2. Approve legitimate horror discussions
3. Reject spam and inappropriate content
4. Respond to engaging comments
5. Pin best comments for visibility

Community Guidelines (Post in channel description):
"📜 Reglas de la Comunidad:
- Respeto mutuo entre todos los miembros
- Sin spam o autopromoción
- Comentarios relevantes al contenido
- Disfruten del terror de forma constructiva
- Los comentarios inapropiados serán eliminados"

Engagement Strategy:
- Like and reply to thoughtful comments
- Answer questions about monsters/folklore
- Thank subscribers for engagement
- Encourage discussion about stories
- Pin comments that add value

Comment Response Templates:
- Positive: "¡Gracias por comentar! ¿Qué te pareció la historia de [monster]?"
- Question: "¿Te gustaría ver más historias de [monster type]?"
- Discussion: "Interesante punto de vista. ¿Conoces otras leyendas similares?"
- Thanks: "¡Gracias por ser parte de nuestra comunidad de terror!"

Moderation Tools:
- YouTube Studio → Comments
- Filter by: Held for review, Likely spam
- Bulk actions: Approve/Reject multiple comments
- Search: Find specific users or keywords

Crisis Management:
- If harassment occurs: Block user immediately
- If spam attack: Enable stricter filters temporarily
- If controversial discussion: Moderate but allow constructive debate
- Report serious violations to YouTube

Analytics for Comments:
- Monitor comment sentiment
- Track most discussed topics
- Identify top community members
- Measure engagement rate
- Adjust moderation based on data

Best Practices:
- Be consistent with moderation
- Respond promptly to comments
- Don't delete constructive criticism
- Learn from community feedback
- Build positive community culture
